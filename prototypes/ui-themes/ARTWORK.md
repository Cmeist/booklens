# Prototype Artwork

Decorative hero assets were created with the built-in image-generation workflow and optimized to
WebP for the local comparison sites. They are original prototype assets, contain no required UI
content, and can be disabled from the Theme Lab.

## Curated Reading Room

Status: retired and removed from the prototype after review. The Curated Reading Room now uses only
small code-native chrome.

Prompt:

> Wide website hero background for BookLens: an original quiet editorial still life suggesting a
> private reading room. A dark forest-green reading table beside softly lit shelves, with stacked
> unbranded books, a brass reading lamp, and faint botanical shadows. Refined painterly editorial
> illustration with subtle paper grain. Wide, calm asymmetry with generous low-detail negative
> space. Warm late-afternoon light. Parchment, library green, muted umber, and restrained brass. No
> people, readable titles, text, logos, trademarks, watermarks, existing artist imitation, or copied
> book-cover design.
